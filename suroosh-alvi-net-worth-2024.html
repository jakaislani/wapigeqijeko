Who is Suroosh Alvi?

Suroosh Alvi is a Canadian journalist, filmmaker, and entrepreneur, best known for co-founding VICE Media, a youth media brand operating in over 50 countries. Alvi’s career has been marked by his commitment to immersive journalism and a desire to push the boundaries of traditional media.

Alvi’s journey began in Montreal, Quebec, where he was born to Pakistani parents. His early interest in music and culture led him to pursue a career in journalism. Along with two of his friends, he founded VICE in 1994 as a punk magazine. Over the years, VICE transformed into a leading global youth media company with a focus on documentary filmmaking, news, and content across various platforms.
