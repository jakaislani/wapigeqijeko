Lamar Joseph Odom, popularly known as Lamar Odom is an American who is a popular former American Basketball Player by profession. As of 2022, Lamar Odom’s net worth is $30 million. He won NBA titles with the Los Angeles Lakers in 2009 and 2010 and was voted the NBA Sixth Man of the Year in 2011.

He competed for the United States national team, earning a bronze medal at the 2004 Summer Olympics and a gold medal in the 2010 FIBA World Championship (later dubbed the World Cup), and is very famous since.

Lamar Odom Wiki/Biography

Born on 6 November 1979, Lamar Odom’s age is 42 years as of 2022. He was born and raised in a well-settled Christian family from South Jamaica, Queens, New York City United States. He is an American by nationality and has a belief in the Christian religion.

He completed his early schooling at St. Thomas Aquinas High School, New Britain, Connecticut.

After that, he enrolled himself at the University of Rhode Island from where he completed his graduation. From the very beginning of his childhood, he was more interested in basketball rather than in studying.
