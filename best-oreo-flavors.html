Best Oreo Flavors: Every Oreo Flavor, Ranked

Publish date: 2024-07-27

If you’re looking for the best Oreo flavors, look no further! We tasted every Oreo flavor currently available to find out which is the best Oreo flavor you can currently buy.

It almost feels like there is an infinite number of Oreo flavors. Every time I thought I had tasted them all, more would appear. Sometimes I would see flavors and couldn’t even tell if they were real or fake! I felt overwhelmed, like I simply couldn’t keep up.

Realizing I probably was not the only person who felt that way, I decided it was our Sporked-ly duty as professional food taste testers to try all the Oreos and let you know which are worth your while. Some were good, some were bad, and a few were perfect.

PS: This list will ebb and flow as Oreo changes their products so check back if you don’t see a new flavor or your current favorite!
