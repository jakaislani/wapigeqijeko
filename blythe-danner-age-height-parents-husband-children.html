Blythe Danner Age, Height, Parents, Husband, Children

Publish date: 2024-08-09

Blythe Katherine Danner is an American actress.

Danner has starred in several films and tv series including Meet the Parents (2000), Meet the Fockers(2004), The Last Kiss (2006), Paul (2011),  I’ll See You in My Dreams (2015), Gypsy (2017), Strange But True (2019), Will & Grace (2001-2020), American Gods (2021), Ridley Jones (2021-2022), and many others.

Blythe Danner is the recipient of two Primetime Emmy Awards and a Tony Award.

Blythe Danner Age

Blythe Danner is currently 79 years old. She was born on February 3, 1943 in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania, United States.

Blythe Danner Height

Blythe Danner stands at a height of 5 feet 7 inches (1.7 m) tall.

Blythe Danner Parents

Blythe Danner’s parents are Katharine Kile Danner and Harry Earl Danner, a bank executive.

Blythe Danner Husband

Blythe Danner was married to director Bruce Weigert Paltrow. Paltrow died on October 3, 2002, at the age of 58, from complications from oral cancer and pneumonia.

Blythe Danner Children

Blythe Danner and director Bruce Paltrow had two children; actress Gwyneth Paltrow and director Jake Paltrow.
