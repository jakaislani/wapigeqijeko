Roasted Vegetable Pasta

Place the sheet with the carrots and onions in the oven first and roast for 30 to 35 minutes, or until the onions are tender and lightly charred and the carrots are tender and browned.   • Garnish with more basil and thyme and a generous drizzle of olive oil.
