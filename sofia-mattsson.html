Sofia Mattsson- Biography

Sofia Mattsson is a successful businesswoman, model, and actress. She recently starred in an ABC TV series called General Hospital, where she acquired a lot of recognition and fan followings on social media platforms.

Sofia Mattsson- Birth, Age, Ethnicity, Siblings

Sofia Mattsson’s Professional Career

She began her acting career in a short film called Shame on Me, in which she played Veronica alongside Olly Murs. In 2012, she starred in Chiddy Bang: Heart Skips a Beat as herself. She appeared in a number of TV shows in 2013, including Two and a Half Men, Total White Guy Move, NCIS, Perception, Jacked Up, and Campus Security. The next year, she appeared in her debut film, Unstrung, in which she played Ninotchka. In 2015, she appeared in Jurassic World as Stephanie and in Hollywood Miles as Roxy in a short film. In 2017, she appeared in various TV episodes and films, including Drive Share, Becoming Bond, Adrenochrome, Love Jacked, Start-Up, and The Therapist.

She landed some of her best parts in 2018 and 2019, including General Hospital, The Wrong Husband, Gothic Harvest, Long Lost Daughter, and My Husband’s Secret Wife. Aside from her professional career, she also owns the AVA CARRINGTON brand, which specialises in handbags and accessories. The products sold are eco-friendly in the sense that they are recyclable, water-resistant, have a low carbon footprint, and are animal-free. She is still working on her ABC TV show General Hospital, where she plays Sasha Gilmore. Aside from her acting profession, she is also expanding and promoting her AVA CARRINGTON handbag and accessories line. She has distinguished herself by making her goods vegan-free and recyclable.
