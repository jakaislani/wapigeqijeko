DeAaron Fox Contract, Stats, Position, Age, Team, Shoes, Wingspan

Publish date: 2024-07-25

De’Aaron Martez Fox is an American professional basketball player for the Sacramento Kings of the National Basketball Association.

He played college basketball for the Kentucky Wildcats before being selected 5th overall by the Kings in the 2017 NBA draft.

De’Aaron Fox Contract

De’Aaron Fox signed a 5-year / $163,000,300 contract with the Sacramento Kings, including $163,000,300 guaranteed, and an annual average salary of $32,600,060. In 2022-23, Fox will earn a base salary of $30,351,780, while carrying a cap hit of $30,351,780 and a dead cap value of $30,351,780.

De’Aaron Fox Stats

De’Aaron Fox has played 6 seasons for the Kings. He has averaged 19.2 points, 6.2 assists and 3.6 rebounds in 325 regular-season games.

De’Aaron Fox Position

De’Aaron Fox plays the point guard position for the Sacramento Kings.

De’Aaron Fox Age

De’Aaron Fox is 24 years old as of 2021. He was born on December 20, 1997 in New Orleans, Louisiana, United States.

De’Aaron Fox Team

De’Aaron Fox currently plays for the Sacramento Kings.

De’Aaron Fox Shoes

De’Aaron Fox Wingspan

De’Aaron Fox’s wingspan is 6’6 (1.98 m).
