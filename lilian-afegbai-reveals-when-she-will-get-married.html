Actress, Lilian Afegbai Reveals When She Will Get Married

Publish date: 2024-07-03

Nigerian actress, Lilian Afegbai has revealed when she will get married.

She made this known during a recent interview with the Saturday Beats and this comes just a few days after she made a revelation that a man with whom she shared mutual attraction stopped wooing her when he realized that she is an actress.

Speaking about marriage in the interview, she said;

“After that incident, I felt more reassured that God will send the right person to me. It was a wake-up call for me to know that what is not mine.

The guy in question is not a bad person. He knows what he wants. It also does not mean I am a bad person because I am an actress. When God says it is time is when I will get married.”

Meanwhile, the actress was in the news not long ago as she revealed that hoodlums have stolen a car’s side mirror.

According to her, she had parked her car outside a building, and on her return, she came to meet her car vandalized by some unidentified men who stole the side mirror and bolted.

In a video that went viral after the incident, one could hear her wailing over the condition of his vandalized automobile.
